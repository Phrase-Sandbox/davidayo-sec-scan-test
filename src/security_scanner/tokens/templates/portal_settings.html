How it works

Storage
Your key is encrypted with AES-256 (Fernet) before being written to the database. The plaintext is held in memory only for the duration of each scan request.
Billing
All LLM calls are made with your key. The provider charges you directly. Visit Usage to see a breakdown and verify against your provider dashboard.
Revoke
Delete your key from your provider's console at any time. All future scans will fail with a 401 until you update the key here.
